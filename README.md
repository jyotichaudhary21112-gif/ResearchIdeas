# 💡 Research Ideas: Materials Theory & Simulation

Welcome to my research repository. This space is dedicated to exploring the fundamental physics that governs material behavior—from the microscopic scales of quantum mechanics to the macroscopic evolution of microstructures.

---

## 🚀 Motivation: What Drives This Research
*The beauty of materials science lies in the "bridge" between the invisible (atoms) and the visible (the physical properties of the materials we build with).*

My research is fueled by three core questions that define my enthusiasm:
1. **Predictive Capability:** Can we accurately predict a material's behavior before it is ever synthesized in a lab?
2. **Structural Evolution:** Why do materials choose to form specific patterns (dendrites, grains) during solidification?
3. **The Micro-Macro Link:** How do quantum-level interactions (calculated via DFT) directly dictate the solidification kinetics and mechanical strength of alloys?

This repository captures the derivations, simulation workflows, and theoretical frameworks that attempt to answer these questions.
